Exchange old books for new

a free website to post your old books and help others connect with you to exchange books

How does this work?
Find a new book
Other users have posted books to the website that you might want to read. See what's available in our database and find contacts to set-up your book exchange.
Offer an old book
Share a posting of your old books to let other users know what's available. This helps motivate others to reach out to you to set up an exchange.
